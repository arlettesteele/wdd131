Tasky! Site Plan

Purpose

Your one-stop platform to find and negotiate home services with ease. The webpage is designed to introduce users to the app’s features, benefits, and supported services while creating a seamless entry point to download the app and get started.

Common Questions

How does Tasky! work?

  • Search for the service you need.
  • Send an offer to the service providers and details.
  • Check if they accepted or want to negotiate the offer.
  • Confirm the booking and get the job done.

What happens if the service I need isn't listed?

You can chat with available Taskers to see if they can provide the service you need.

What types of services are available on Tasky!?

  • Cleaning
  • Home Repairs
  • Babysitting
  • Electrical Work
  • Plumbing
  • Gardering and Yard Maintenance
  • And More!

How do I know the service providers are trustworthy?

All Tasky! providers are verified, and customer reviews are displayed on their profiles to help you make informed decisions.

Is Tasky! free to use?

Tasky! is free to download and use for finding service providers. Service costs are negotiated directly with providers.

Can I negotiate the price for services?

Yes! Tasky! allows you to chat directly with service providers to discuss pricing and finalize agreements.

What happens if I'm not satisfied with the service?

Tasky! encourages users to leave reviews and ratings. If there’s an issue, contact the provider directly or reach out to Tasky! support for assistance.

How do I pay for services?

Payment methods vary depending on the provider. Many offer secure in-app payment options or accept cash upon completion.

How can I become a service provider on Tasky!?

Sign up through the Tasky! app and complete the verification process to start offering your services.
