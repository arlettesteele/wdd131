Reedefined Smiles Site Plan

Purpose

This page serves as a simple and welcoming online presence, providing basic information such as office hours, location, contact details, and available services. It helps new and existing patients learn about the practice, meet the staff, and easily schedule appointments.

Do you accept insurance?

Yes! We accept most major dental insurance plans. Our team can help verify your coverage and explain your benefits before your appointment. If you're unsure whether we accept your plan, feel free to call us. For treatments not fully covered by insurance, we also offer flexible payment options to make your care more affordable.

How can I make appointments?

You can schedule an appointment by calling our office at during business hours. Walk-ins are welcome based on availability, but we recommend booking in advance to secure your preferred time. If you need to reschedule or cancel, please let us know at least 24hrs in advance.

Do you accept children?

Yes! We welcome patients of all ages, including children. Our team is experienced in providing gentle, kid-friendly dental care to help children feel comfortable and develop good oral health habits.
